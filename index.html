About

The names and surnames are randomly generated among the 300 most frequent names and surnames in Spain, according to the latest data provided by the National Institute of Statistics (INE), updated until 01/01/2021.

Avatars are randomly chosen from 100 pre-made avatars, using NVIDIA's StyleGAN random person face generation artificial intelligence, at this-person-does-not-exist.com.
